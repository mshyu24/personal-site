# Mango Salsa

## Ingredients

- 2 ripe mangoes, chopped
- 1/2 bunch cilantro, chopped
- 2 jalapenos, minced
- 1 red onion, chopped finely
- Juice of 2 limes
- Freshly ground pepper
- Salt

## Instructions

Mix chopped produce in a bowl. Pour lime juice over and mix. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
